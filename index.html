Cardinal Eats Magazine

The intersection of food, writing, design, and photography

Cardinal Eats is Iowa State's Food Magazine. Our goal is to bring recipies and the stories that encompass them to our readers. We also provide a great way for Journalism, Graphic Design, and any other major to get expirence in the following skills as well as bring tangible work to interviews.

If you want to gain expirence in the Adobe Suite and learn about how to format and compose a magazine, this is a great way to get that expirence in a low stress environment. Cardinal Eats is a great way to spice up your portfolio.
